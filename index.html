Pixel art is a type of art which (seeing its name) is a type of digital art art made of pixels. Well... all art is made by pixels (except vector art) but pixel art is made with a limited amount of pixels.

You might be able to notice each pixel separately. An art in this low resolution is called PIXEL ART

You might also see a lot of games having a pixelated style. For example Minecraft, Pac-man, super Mario bros.,etc.
